4. Product Bundling: Collaborate with complementary businesses to create a bundle offer. For example, a fitness app could partner with a healthy meal delivery service. This provides added value to customers and encourages them to make a purchase.

5. Referral Programs: Encourage your existing customers to refer their friends and family in return for incentives such as discounts, free products, or exclusive access. This not only attracts new customers but also boosts customer loyalty.

6. Guest Posting: Write a blog post for a partner's website or vice versa. This can help you reach a new audience, build backlinks to improve SEO, and establish your brand as an industry authority.

Remember to choose partners that align with your brand values and have a good reputation among your target audience. Monitor the performance of each partnership and continually optimize for better results.
